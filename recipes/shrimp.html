Classic Lemon Garlic Butter Shrimp

Description

This dish is the ultimate "emergency" dinner that tastes like a five-star meal. In less than 15 minutes, you get succulent shrimp bathed in a rich, zesty butter sauce that begs for a side of crusty bread or a bed of linguine.

It’s a crowd-pleaser that balances the sharpness of fresh garlic with the brightness of citrus. Whether you're cooking for a date night or just treating yourself after a long Tuesday, this recipe never misses.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb large shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 3 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Juice of half a lemon
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Pat the shrimp dry with paper towels and season them lightly with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the olive oil and 1 tablespoon of butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the shrimp in a single layer and cook for about 2 minutes per side until pink and opaque. Remove shrimp from the pan and set aside.
  4. Lower the heat to medium. Add the remaining butter and the minced garlic to the same skillet. Sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Stir in the lemon juice and red pepper flakes, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the pan.
  6. Toss the shrimp back into the pan to coat them in the sauce.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve immediately.
