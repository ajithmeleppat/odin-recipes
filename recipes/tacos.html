Roasted Sweet Potato & Black Bean Tacos

Description

These tacos are a vibrant explosion of textures and flavors, combining the natural sweetness of caramelized potatoes with the earthy heartiness of black beans. It's a healthy, filling meal that feels indulgent.

The secret is all in the roasting process, which gives the sweet potatoes those crispy edges that hold up perfectly against a soft tortilla. Top them with a squeeze of lime to cut through the richness of the avocado.

Ingredients

  • 2 large sweet potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1 can (15 oz) black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced
  • Fresh cilantro and lime wedges

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Toss the cubed sweet potatoes on a baking sheet with olive oil, cumin, paprika, and salt.
  3. Roast for 20–25 minutes, flipping halfway through, until the potatoes are tender and browned.
  4. While the potatoes roast, warm the black beans in a small saucepan over medium heat with a splash of water.
  5. Warm the tortillas in a dry skillet or directly over a low gas flame until pliable.
  6. Assemble the tacos by layering the roasted sweet potatoes and black beans onto the tortillas.
  7. Top with avocado slices, cilantro, and a generous squeeze of lime juice.
